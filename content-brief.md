# Content Brief — ManoSthir

## Overall Message
ManoSthir is a safe, culturally sensitive space for professional psychological support that bridges modern evidence-based therapy with deep cultural understanding — available to anyone, anywhere in the world.

## Tone of Voice
- [x] Warm and nurturing
- [x] Professional and authoritative
- [ ] Casual and down-to-earth
- [ ] Spiritual/holistic
- [ ] Direct and practical

Specific tone: Warm, empathetic, culturally aware. Professional but never cold. Uses "we" and "you" language. Acknowledges the courage it takes to seek help. Feels like a wise, compassionate friend who also happens to be highly qualified.

## Key Phrases from the Designs
- "A safe space for your mind to heal and grow"
- "Guided by empathy, grounded in science"
- "Human connection is the heart of healing"
- "Professional global online therapy tailored to your unique journey"
- "We bridge the gap between deep-rooted Indian values and global therapeutic standards"
- "A sanctuary for healing and self-discovery"
- "Take the first step toward emotional wellbeing"
- "We are here to support your journey, wherever you are in the world"
- "Healing should fit into your life, not the other way around"
- "Begin your journey toward mental clarity"
- "Compassionate psychological support for the modern life"

## What Should NOT Be on the Website
- Clinical jargon without explanation
- Stigmatizing language about mental illness
- Stock photos of random people — use real photos of Moumita
- Promises of cures or guaranteed outcomes
- Comparison to or criticism of other therapists/practices
